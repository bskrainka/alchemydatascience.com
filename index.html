About Us

Our founder, Benjamin S. Skrainka, has been a data scientists since long before data scientists roamed the earth. As an undergraduate, by writing software to study the curvature of space-time geometry and crater relaxation on icy moons, he realized that software engineering was fundamental tool for modern science. Today, he brings the toolkits of social and natural science to bear on relevant applied problems complemented by industrial-strength engineering methods.

We understand that successful data science projects must solve the right problem to the appropriate degree of accuracy and also be deployed in your technology ecosystem on-time and on-budget. Consequently, our approach combines the industry leading data science workflow with a framework for evaluating model correctness. And, we drive this project using whichever software engineering methodology you prefer. This approach produces good results which solve the right business question and actually work in the real world.

WE BELIEVE IN GREAT SCIENCE

Great science depends on good workflow, such as CRISP-DM, to ensure consistency and reproducibility — from solving the right business problem to appropriate models to deployment.

Great science achieves correctness through verification, validation, and uncertainty quantification (VV & UQ).

Great science is built by using modern software engineering methodologies such as Agile or Kanban.

We combine these three frameworks, each of which is necessary for success, to produce great science-driven results for our clients.
